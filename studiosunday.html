Who We Are

Born from the belief that everyone is creative.

Studio Sunday is Mississauga's roaming creative social club. We don't have walls — we have wheels. Our instructors show up wherever you are: your home, your backyard, your office, your event venue.

Starting with clay hand-building, we're expanding into multi-medium creative nights — think macramé, painting, candle-making, and more. Every session is equal parts art class and social gathering.

"Our mission is to make hands-on creativity accessible, joyful, and communal — no experience required, just curiosity."
